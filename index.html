    About Us

    We are a FAITH based family first resource and recovery house. We provide stable housing and recovery services for our inhouse guests. We also can provide several other services for outside residents based on a referral voucher program for single parent households. Our goal is to educate, rehabilitate, and motivate our families to become stronger, happier, and healthier. We are succeeding one step at a time!
